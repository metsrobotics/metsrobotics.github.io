ACCESS PROTOCOLS (FAQ)

Do I need to know coding to join?

No. We welcome all skill levels. Whether you are a master coder or just curious about how things move, we have a place for you. We teach from scratch.

Is the club open to Mechanical/Civil students?

Yep. Robotics is multidisciplinary. We need mechanical designers for bodies, civil engineers for structural integrity, and electrical engineers for power. Everyone is welcome.
